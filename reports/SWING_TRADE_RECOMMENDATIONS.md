## RISK MANAGEMENT FRAMEWORK

### Position Sizing
- **Best setups** (R:R >3x, Conf 7/10): 3-5% risk per trade
- **Good setups** (R:R 1.5-3x, Conf 6/10): 2% risk per trade
- **Speculative** (POET, NBIS): 1% max (biotech binary risk)

### Stop Loss Rules
- Always place stop below identified support (MA20 or low_20)
- Use ATR-based stops if MA-based too wide: Stop = Entry - (1.5 × ATR)
- Move stop to break-even after +2% gain

### Profit-Taking
- **Take 30% off at Target 1** (lock in edge)

- **Trail stop after +5%** gain if momentum accelerates

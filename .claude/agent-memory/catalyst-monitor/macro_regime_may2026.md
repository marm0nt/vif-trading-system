Fed held rates at 3.5-3.75% at April 29 FOMC (8-4 split — unusual dissent). Forecasts 1 cut in 2026.
Next FOMC: June 17, 2026.

**Why:** April meeting had 4 dissenters — hawkish faction (Hammack, Kashkari, Logan) against easing bias; one dovish dissenter (Miran) wanted a cut. Highest dissent count since October 1992. Signals policy uncertainty.

**How to apply:** Rate-sensitive sectors (REITs, utilities, long-duration growth) remain volatile around Fed dates. Do not initiate new positions in DLR, TRNO, LXP, NEE, CEG in the 2 days before June 17.

US-China chip tariff truce: New China semiconductor tariffs delayed to mid-2027. China removed antitrust/anti-dumping investigations on US semicon firms. Tariff on Taiwanese goods capped at 15%.

US semiconductor Section 232 tariffs: 25% on narrow category of re-exported advanced chips (effective Jan 15 2026). Review scheduled July 1, 2026 — potential policy change catalyst.
